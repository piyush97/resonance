## PART 3: CHOOSING YOUR VERTICAL (Critical Decision)

### If You Build AI Chatbot, Which Vertical First?

```
VERTICAL ANALYSIS:

1. B2B SaaS (RECOMMENDED)
   Pain point: "Customers expect 24/7, our support team burns out"
   Typical spend: $50K-150K/month on support
   Decision speed: Fast (technical founders get it immediately)
   Pricing you can charge: $500-2K/month
   Sales cycle: 2-4 weeks
   Annual revenue per customer: $6K-24K
   
   Win strategy:
   - Target: SaaS companies with 50-500 employees
   - ICP: Support cost is visible pain, not hidden
   - Channel: Direct outreach to support managers
   - Offer: Try free for 14 days, measure cost savings

2. E-commerce
   Pain point: "High volume, low margin, support costs eating profits"
   Typical spend: $100K-500K/month on support
   Decision speed: Slow (retail bureaucracy, RFP process)
   Pricing you can charge: $1K-5K/month
   Sales cycle: 4-12 weeks
   Annual revenue per customer: $12K-60K
   
   Challenge: Margins are thin, hard to justify new spend
   Advantage: Huge volume (many small e-commerce companies)

3. Fintech
   Pain point: "Need compliance audit trail, security, 24/7"
   Typical spend: $200K-1M+/month on support
   Decision speed: Very slow (compliance review, security audit)
   Pricing you can charge: $5K-20K+/month
   Sales cycle: 12-24 weeks
   Annual revenue per customer: $60K-240K+
   
   Challenge: Long sales cycles, regulatory approval needed
   Advantage: Huge budgets once you're approved

VERDICT: Start with B2B SaaS
- Fastest to first customer
- Easiest to test value prop
- Repeat playbook to $1M ARR
- THEN expand to other verticals
```
