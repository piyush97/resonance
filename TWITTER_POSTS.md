## Tips for Twitter

1. **Keep it under 280 chars**
2. **Use emojis** (saves characters, adds visual interest)
3. **One idea per tweet**
4. **Use threads** for longer content
5. **Add media** (images, GIFs, videos)
6. **Ask questions** (drives engagement)
7. **Use 2-3 hashtags max**
8. **Post consistently** (daily)
